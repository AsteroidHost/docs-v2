### Understanding the Docs

- [Blue Text](#) are embedded links. These will link to

  - Other steps

- ***On the left sidebar*** there are categories. If you click on the arrow it will reveal the relevant guides for that category.

- Hovering your mouse over a title *(such as the "Understanding the Docs" above)*, will show a blue **[#](#)** icon. Clicking it will create and copy a link to that spot in the guide to share.
